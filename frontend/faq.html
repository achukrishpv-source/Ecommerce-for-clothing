Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to your most common questions

🔍

How long does delivery take?

+

Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days. Express delivery is available and takes 1–2 business days. Same-day delivery is available in select cities for orders placed before 12PM.

How can I track my order?

+

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive an SMS and email with the tracking link. You can also track your order from your profile page or visit our order tracking page with your Order ID.

Do you deliver across India?

+

Yes! We deliver pan-India to over 27,000 PIN codes. Free delivery is available on all orders above ₹999. For remote areas, delivery may take 5–7 business days.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

+

You can cancel or modify your order within 2 hours of placing it by contacting our support team. Once the order is packed, modifications may not be possible. Cancellations after dispatch are subject to our return policy.

What is your return policy?

+

We offer a hassle-free 30-day return policy. Items must be unused, unwashed, and in original packaging with tags intact. Sale items are eligible for exchange only.

How do I initiate a return or exchange?

+

Go to your Order History, select the order, and click "Return/Exchange". Our team will arrange a pickup within 2 business days. Refunds are processed within 5–7 days after the item is received.

When will I receive my refund?

+

Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after we receive the returned item. For UPI and cards, the amount reflects in 3–5 days. Bank transfers may take up to 7 days.

How do I find the right size?

+

Each product page has a detailed size guide with measurements. We recommend measuring your chest, waist, and hips and comparing with our size chart. If you're between sizes, we suggest going one size up for comfort.

What fabric/material is used?

+

ASKR uses only premium materials — 100% cotton, premium linens, stretch denim, and high-quality blends. All fabric details are mentioned on individual product pages under "Product Details".

Are the product colors accurate in photos?

+

We take great care to accurately represent product colors. However, slight color variations may occur due to screen calibration differences. If you're unsatisfied with the color, our return policy applies.

What payment methods are accepted?

+

We accept UPI (PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm, BHIM), Credit Cards, Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Rupay, Amex), Net Banking, and Cash on Delivery (₹40 COD fee applies).

Is it safe to pay on ASKR?

+

Absolutely! All transactions on ASKR are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption. We are PCI DSS compliant and use 3D Secure authentication for card payments. Your financial data is never stored on our servers.

How do I create an account?

+

Click "Sign Up" on the login page and fill in your name, email, mobile number, and password. You can also sign up instantly with Google or Facebook. Creating an account lets you track orders, manage wishlists, and access exclusive offers.

I forgot my password. What should I do?

+

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your registered email and you'll receive a password reset link within 2 minutes. Check your spam folder if you don't see it in your inbox.
