📖 The Beginning

Our Story

ASKR Clothing was founded in 2020 with a singular vision — to make premium fashion accessible to every Indian. What started as a small design studio in Bengaluru has grown into one of India's most loved fashion brands.

Our founder, Ashwath, believed that great style shouldn't come at an unaffordable price. He combined his passion for streetwear aesthetics with Indian craftsmanship to create ASKR — a brand that truly understands the modern Indian.

Today, with over 50,000 happy customers and a growing family, ASKR continues to push boundaries in fashion, quality, and customer experience.
