📖 The Beginning

Our Story

ASKR Clothing was founded in 2025 with a singular vision — to make premium fashion accessible to every Indian. Born in the heart of Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, ASKR set out to redefine what modern Indian fashion looks and feels like.

Our founder, Ashwath Krishna, believed that great style shouldn't come at an unaffordable price. He combined his passion for streetwear aesthetics with Indian craftsmanship to create ASKR — a brand that truly understands the modern Indian.

In just months since launch, ASKR has earned thousands of happy customers across India and continues to push boundaries in fashion, quality, and customer experience.
